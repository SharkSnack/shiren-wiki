Collecting Points

Destiny Trail Trap Bracelets

  1. Obtain 2 Trap Bracelets. (See Rescue Passwords)
  2. Equip a weapon and shield set that grants 2 bracelet resonance, and equip the 2 Trap Bracelets.
  3. Go to Destiny Trail and collect points until the 3rd wind warning on both 1F and 2F.
    ※ Don't forget to bring an Escape Scroll.
  4. Read an Escape Scroll, and repeat as needed.

Strengthen Equipment

Upgrade Value

Undergound Manor - District 4 (31~40F)

Repeat district 4 to take home a Dotanuki+8 and Wolfshead+10 each run, and then synthesize them.

Blessed Fate + Earth + Extraction Scrolls

  1. Be able to write Blessing and Extraction on Blank Scrolls.
    (See Tanuki Passwords for Blessing Scroll, and Rescue Passwords for Extraction Scroll)
  2. Prepare 3 pots with 5 Fate, 5 Earth, and 5 Extraction scrolls inside.
    (Blank Scroll = 200 points, use the Destiny Trail Trap Bracelet method)
  3. Exchange for 2 more Blank Scrolls, and prepare a blessed Extraction Scroll.
  4. Use the blessed Extraction Scroll on the 5 Extraction Scrolls pot.
  5. Use the blessed Extraction Scrolls on the Fate or Earth scroll pots as desired.
    (Save one blessed Extraction Scroll to bless another set of 5 Extraction Scrolls for efficiency)

Equipment Level

Blessed Gambler's + Extraction Scrolls

  1. Be able to write Gambler's, Blessing, and Extraction on Blank Scrolls.
    (See Tanuki Passwords for Gambler's and Blessing scrolls, and Rescue Passwords for Extraction Scroll)
  2. Prepare many pots filled with Gambler's Scrolls, and a pot with 5 Extraction Scrolls inside.
    (Blank Scroll = 200 points, use the Destiny Trail Trap Bracelet method)
  3. Exchange for 2 more Blank Scrolls, and prepare a blessed Extraction Scroll.
  4. Use the blessed Extraction Scroll on the 5 Extraction Scrolls pot.
  5. Use the blessed Extraction Scrolls on the Gambler's Scroll pots.
    (Save one blessed Extraction Scroll to bless another set of 5 Extraction Scrolls for efficiency)
  6. Fill your inventory with weapons and shields you want to level up, and go to Destiny Trail 5F.
    ※ Don't forget to bring an Escape Scroll.
  7. Read the Gambler's Scrolls, aiming for the effect that increases the level of all equipment in your inventory by 1.
    ※ Equipment in pots won't level up.

Miscellaneous Knowledge

Rune Priority

Some runes take priority over others in Shiren 5.

Flame Shot + Blue Flame
Blue Flame.
It also works with Burning Blade, but you'll deal less damage at Lv7~8 (Lv7: 21→20, Lv8: 25→20).
Burning Blade + Swap Shield resonance deals 30 damage at all times with Blue Flame.
Expendable + Rustproof
Expendable.
However, Rustproof still prevents rust from Rust Traps and Mudkin monsters.
Focusing + Redeeming or Accurate
Focusing.
Diurnal + Nocturnal
Both runes vanish if you try to synthesize them on a piece of equipment.
Anti-Blast + Anti-Blast※
Anti-Blast※
Anti-Hypno or Magi-Twister + Redirecting※
Redirecting※
Offensive + Costly (0 Gitan)
Shield defense is added to attack like normal.
