※ means enter a dungeon and return.

  1. Talk to the girl near Nekomaneki Village's storage entrance →※→ Talk to her again.
  2. Talk to Decchi near the peach spring (north west) →※→ Talk to him again (south west)
  3. Pray to the 6 cat statues inside the village. (Point Shop, Dungeon Center, Hotel)
  4. Check the large cat statue to receive Revival Grass →※
  5. Talk to Decchi (south east) to receive Undo Grass →※
  6. Talk to the girl to learn that Decchi went to Inori Village →※
  7. Talk to Decchi (west) in Inori Village →※→ Talk to him again (east)
  8. Clap twice at all 7 pots in the village → Check the well to receive Kabura Katana →※
  9. The Onigiri Hollow unlock event plays when you return to Inori Village.
