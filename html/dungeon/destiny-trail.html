Shiren 5 / Destiny Trail

Destiny Trail

First non-tutorial dungeon of the game.
There are 5 floors total, and clearing this dungeon leads to the entrance of Tower of Fortune.

The first 2 floors only have Point Switches for traps, so it's a popular spot to collect points.
