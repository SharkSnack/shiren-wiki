Staff Summary

Name Buy +1 Sell +1 Ct Rune Info
Swap Staff 600 30 210 10.5 5~7 - Swap places with a target.
If you swap places with a target over water or a void, you'll warp.
Knockback Staff - Send a target flying 10 tiles, dealing 5 damage if it collides with something.
If they land on a water / void tile and can't walk on water / float, they'll warp.
Pinning Staff - Leap forward toward whatever the magic bullet hit.
If you land on a water or void tile, you'll warp.
Slow Staff 4~6 - Decreases movement speed of a target by 1 stage.
Swift Staff Quick Hitting* Increase movement speed of a target by 1 stage.
Mage Staff 5~7 - Inflicts Asleep, Confused, Slow, or warps the target to a different location.
Transient Staff 900 45 315 15.75 4~6 - Warps a target to the stairs and inflicts Paralyzed status.
Paralysis Staff Paralyzing Inflicts the target with Paralyzed status.
Empathy Staff 5~7 - Inflicts the target with Empathetic status.
Trap Del. Staff - Erases all traps the magic bullet travels over.
Deals 2 damage if the magic bullet hits a creature.
Balance Staff - Prevents tripping on Trip Traps, but no effect if it's inside a pot.
-1 use every time it activates, and no effect once it reaches 0.
Ordinary Staff Anti-Magic The magic bullet has no effect.
Drama Staff 4~6 - Enjoy a short play when swung.
Inflicts the target with Blind status if thrown.
Nagging Staff - Receive a lecture when you trip on a Trip Trap.
Electric Staff 1200 60 420 21 - Deals 25 damage to a target.
The attack travels through all adjacent creatures.
Shocking Staff - Deals 25 damage to yourself.
The attacks travels through all adjacent creatures.
Seal Staff 1500 75 525 26.25 Sealing Inflicts the target with Sealed status.
Clone Staff - Inflicts the target with Cloned status.
Staff of Sacrifice - Get hit by a log coming from the left side of the direction you're facing.
The log deals 10 tiles of knockback.
Glorious Staff 1800 90 630 31.5 - Increase the target's level by 1. (Gain 500 exp if target is Shiren or an ally)
Unlucky Staff - Decrease the target's level by 1.
Boring Staff - Digs a tunnel 10 tiles deep if the magic bullet hits a wall.
Deals 10 damage if the magic bullet hits a creature.
Fort. Staff Anti-Blast* Create a wall 1 tile in front of you.
The wall can be destroyed by the player using a regular attack.
Deals 2 damage if thrown at a creature.
Sharing Staff Dispersing Inflicts yourself with Empathetic status toward the target.
