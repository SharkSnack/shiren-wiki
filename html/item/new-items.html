Shiren 5 / New Items

New Items

New Items is a unique mechanic of Shiren 5 where you use a base item to create an item with added abilities.

Mechanics

Creating New Items

Insert an item into a secret pot located in the basement of Hotel Nekomaneki, and wait for random abilities to get added.
The abilities are added at midnight, and change each day for 7 days. Once it reaches day 8, the item turns into Weeds.

New Items can be created from weapons, shields, bracelets, grass, staves, scrolls, and talismans.
Other item categories like pots, food, arrows, rocks, and torches can't be inserted into secret pots.

New Items are automatically registered when they're taken out of a secret pot, and up to 64 items can be registered at a time.
Registered items can't be erased, with the exception of the oldest item being erased when you exceed the 64 item limit.

They also appear in some dungeons that don't allow carry-in items, so they can easily break the balance of the game.
Therefore, it's highly recommended to make two save files - one with New Items, and one without.

Time Adjustments

The idea is to adjust console time to skip having to wait until the next day in real time.
Some players feel using these methods is cheating, so take that into account whether you perform them or not.
(However, Shiren 5 is primarily a single-player game, so you'd only be souring your own experience)

DS

Simply advance the console time 1 day at a time, checking the secret pot after each change.
※ If you try to return to a previous date, the item will turn into Weeds.

PS Vita

It's possible to change the date while playing, including both advancing and returning to previous dates.

Example:
Insert an item on 7/10, and while on that screen, press the PS button → change date to 7/17 in settings → check the secret pot.
If you don't like the result, you can go back 1 day and check the secret pot on 7/16, all the way back to 7/11.
If you then feel like the result on 7/14 was the best, you can change the date back to that date and register that item.
Perhaps you didn't like any of them, in which case you can take out the item on 7/11 (if unchanged) and reinsert the item.

That said, if any of the dates of inserted items go in the negative, all items will turn into Weeds.
So it's best to insert items into secret pots at the same time when they're all empty, rather than inserting across different dates.

For those that have a PS Plus subscription or a PC and don't mind more blatant cheating,
you can create a backup of your save data before inserting items, and reload if you didn't like any of the results.
This lets you retry immediately without having to spend time strengthening equipment or reaquiring rare items.

Stats and Abilities

  • The base item's upgrade value, runes, use count, blessing, etc. are ignored.
    Stats and abilities are returned to their base values the moment the item is inserted.
  • The abilities that get added are selected at random, and some abilities are more common than others.
    This can easily be seen when you insert bracelets, where cheaper bracelet abilities like Bunch Bracelet
    or Can. Arm Bracelet are common, but abilities like Wall Clip Bracelet or VIP Bracelet are very rare.
  • Scrolls and staves have categories for abilities, and only same category abilities will get added.
    • Scrolls are split into selection and non-selection abilities.
      Effects like Fate Scroll will never get added to a base item that doesn't have a selection such as a Confusion Scroll,
      and selection-based scrolls will only gain selection-based abilities.
    • Staves are split into those that fire magic bullets, and those that don't.
      Staves that fire magic bullets will only gain abilities that fire magic bullets, and vice versa.
  • The number of added abilities can increase as days pass, up to a max of 3 abilities.
    • The number of added abilities will never decrease as days pass.
    • Multiple abilities can get added in a single day.
      Example: Two days without any added abilities, and then suddenly gain 3 abilities the next day.
  • Many added abilities are based on existing items, but some abilities are exclusive to New Items.
    • Some existing item abilities will never get added to New Items, such as Floating Bracelet.
      So if you want a New Item bracelet with the Floating Bracelet effect, you have to insert a Floating Bracelet.
  • Weapon and shield stats have variance.
    • Base attack or defense can have a max increase of +15.
    • Upgrade limit and rune slot count are included in this variance.
    • There are 6 outcomes when it rolls for stats. (Bad, Bad Rare, Normal, Normal Rare, Good, Good Rare)
      If the previous day rolled Bad, the next day can roll Bad, Bad Rare, Normal, Normal Rare, Good, or Good Rare.
      If the previous day rolled Normal, the next day can only roll Normal, Normal Rare, Good, or Good Rare.
      If the previous day rolled Normal Rare, the next day can only roll Normal Rare or Good Rare.
      So basically, stats won't get worse as days pass.
      ※ Upgrade limit and rune slot count have ranges for each roll rather than a set value, so you might see them decrease.
      　Example using Ordinary Stick's upgrade value: Bad = 50~55, Normal = 53~58, so if you roll Bad and get 55,
      　and then roll a Normal and get 53 the next day, the value decreased despite being a better roll.
    • Bad, Normal, and Good rolls are shared between base attack or defense, upgrade limit, rune slot count, and ability count.
      On the other hand, base attack or defense, upgrade limit, and rune slot count have their own rolls for Rare.
    • For ability count, Bad = 0~1, Normal = 2, and Good = 3
    • Lastly, the chance for better rolls increases as days pass, and inserting higher level equipment also boosts said chance.
  • Possible values for weapon and shield stats are based on equipment level, so always insert Lv8 equipment.
  • Resonance is based on the base item. (example: Strength Bracelet as a base, resonates with Counter Shield)
  • Inserting a Fixer Scroll as a base allows you to read the New Item scorll without light at night.
