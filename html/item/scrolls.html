Shiren 5 / Scrolls

Scroll Summary

Name Buy Sell Rune Info
Wet Scroll 10 3 - Scroll that became wet, and the item is lost if read.
Chance to dry and return to normal when you change floors.
Piece of Paper - No effect.
Escape Scroll 100 35 - Escape from the dungeon with your items and Gitan.
Navigation Scroll - Reveals the current floor's layout, item and monster locations.
Oil Scroll Blue Flame* No effect if read, and sticks to the ground when placed.
Inflicts Tottering status to the enemy who walks on it.
Flames up and deals 50 damage if the enemy is a Floating type.
Light Scroll - No effect if read, and sticks to the ground when placed.
Lights up the room at night when placed in a room.
Recommend. Letter - Show it to an elite store shopkeeper to enter the store.
No effect if read.
Commend. Letter - Read it or show it to an elite store shopkeeper to buy a Large Onigiri.
However, you'll lose all of your Gitan.
Identify Scroll 200 70 - Identifies a selected item.
Small chance to identify all items not inside pots.
Can be given to a shopkeeper to identify all merchandise.
Gathering Scroll 300 105 - Gathers allies present on the current floor.
Gathers monsters present on the floor if there are no allies.
Collection Scroll - Gathers items present on the current floor.
Merchandise inside stores cannot be gathered.
Squid Sushi Scroll - Can be eaten to restore 40 fullness. No effect if read.
Exorcism Scroll 500 175 - Remove a curse or seal from a selected item.
Small chance to remove curses or seals from all items not inside pots.
Deals 100 damage if thrown at Curse Girls.
Fate Scroll - Increases the selected weapon's upgrade value by 1.
Small chance to increase upgrade value by 3.
Removes any curse or seal from the selected weapon.
Earth Scroll - Increases the selected shield's upgrade value by 1.
Small chance to increase upgrade value by 3.
Removes any curse or seal from the selected shield.
Plating Scroll - Adds the Rustproof rune to the selected weapon or shield.
Removes any curse or seal from the selected weapon or shield.
Tag Scroll - Places a tag on the selected weapon or shield.
Sale Scroll - Transforms the selected item into Gitan equal to its selling price.
Onigiri Scroll - Transforms the selected item into a Large Onigiri.
Pot God Scroll - Increases the size of a selected pot by 1, up to a max size of 5.
Extraction Scroll - Extracts the contents of a pot onto the ground.
Blessing Scroll - Blesses the selected item.
Curse Scroll - Curses or seals the selected item.
Coupon Scroll - Removes the price tag of all merchandise inside a store.
Mate Scroll - A future mate appears and grants one of the following effects:
Halve HP, Restore HP and status, obtain 1000 Gitan,
obtain an item, or wipe out all monsters on the current floor.
Darth Scroll - Increases upgrade value of a weapon or shield that isn't the selected item.
Small chance to increase upgrade value by 3.
Removes any curse or seal from the upgraded weapon or shield.
Pot Dog Scroll - Summons 4 Absorbiphants around the player.
Fear Scroll 800 280 - Inflicts Afraid status on monsters within a 1 tile radius.
Trap Deletion Scrl - Erases all traps on the current floor.
Desert Scroll Anti-Aquatic All water tiles change to ground tiles on the current floor.
Trap Scroll - Creates lots of new traps on the current floor.
Muzzled Scroll - Inflicts Muzzled status on the player.
Grounded Scroll - Inflicts Grounded status on the player.
Attraction Scroll - Gathers allies present on the current floor.
If there are no allies, gathers either items or monsters instead.
Confusion Scroll 1000 350 - Inflicts Confused status on monsters in the room.
Slumber Scroll - Inflicts Sound Asleep status on monsters in the room.
Vacuum Slash Scrl - Deals an average of 40~45 damage to monsters in the room.
Dispel Aura Scroll - Removes auras from monsters on the current floor.
Monsters will no longer spawn with auras on the current floor.
Night-Day Scroll - Switches the time of day. (Day → Night, or Night → Day)
Swift Foe Scroll - Increases the movement speed of monsters in the room by 1 stage.
Mnster House Scrl - Turns the current room into a Monster House.
If read in a hallway, you'll be warped to a random room first.
If one is already present, the next floor will be a Monster House.
Immunity Scroll Anti-Blast* Grants Immune status, making you and allies immune to ailments.
Also cures any existing ailments when read.
Replenish Scroll Ability Regen Makes all previously used abilities usable.
Fixer Scroll - Fully restores HP, and grants an additional effect based on situation.
Can be read at night without light.
Gambler's Scroll - One of the following random effects occur:
Increase level by 10, warp 5 floors, exterminate a random monster,
Hasten all monsters by 2 stages, level up all equipment by 1 stage,
lose all of your Gitan, or the player explodes.
Arbor Scroll - No effect. (Heals a damaged branch somewhere in the world)
Nixer Scroll - 50% chance to obtain a monster license if read in front of a monster.
Receive damage equal to 50% of your current HP if it fails.
Lost Scroll 3000 1050 Bored Makes you forget the current floor's layout.
Sanctuary Scroll - No effect if read, and sticks to the ground when placed.
Standing on this scroll stops monsters from attacking you directly.
If a second Sanctuary Scroll is placed, the first one vanishes.
You can pick it up and reuse it only if the scroll was blessed.
Expulsion Scroll - Wipes out all monsters in the room. (No experience points)
If read in a hallway, wipes out adjacent monsters.
Bankruptcy Scroll - Makes you lose all of your Gitan.
Blank Scroll 5000 1750 - Write the name of a scroll you've read before, and use this as that scroll.
Extinction Scroll 10000 3500 - Throw at a monster to make that monster family vanish from the adventure.
If another scroll is thrown, the first family will begin to appear again.
Shopkeepers and Maneaters will still appear after being hit by this scroll.
The scroll will land on the ground if it misses. (Unlike Shiren 4)
