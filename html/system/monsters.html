Night Monsters

  • Only receive 1 damage from non-ability attacks.
    (Equipment resonance and Night Ward can increase damage dealt)
  • Susceptible to statuses like day monsters, but are immune to Berserk status.
  • Erratic movement, but they unmistakably get closer to Shiren if he's in the room.
  • Not 100%, but very likely to attack when a creature is adjacent to them.
