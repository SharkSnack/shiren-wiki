Necklace Abilities

Necklace abilities, also called techniques, are attacks that can be used only at night.
They are the primary means to deal damage to night monsters,
and are first used in Fortune Tower during the main story.

Up to 8 abilities can be assigned to the necklace, including multiples of the same ability.
Roaming NPCs can teach you new abilities, and you can swap abilities at Hotel Nekomaneki.
Some abilities can only be learned by repeatedly using specific abilities.

With the exception of Staccato Wave, abilities can only be used one time per floor.
It's possible to replenish moves by eating peaches, reading a Replenish Scroll,
being revived by Revival Grass / Undo Grass, or the Ability Regen rune taking effect.

Attack power of abilities increases as Shiren levels up or gains the Super status.
Most abilities deal 2 damage to shopkeepers, but status effects still work normally.
