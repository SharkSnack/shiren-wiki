Blessings / Curses / Seals

  • Blessed items are worth 10% more Gitan, and cursed or sealed items are worth 80% of their regular value.
  • Blessed bracelets and pots are protected from being cursed, sealed, or turned into an onigiri.
    The blessing has a chance to wear off: "But X was fine because it was blessed." - Curse Trap, Curse Scroll, Nigiri Morph
    The blessing won't wear off: "...But the Curse was ineffective." - Curse Girls
  • Other item categories don't appear to have resistance against being sealed, even when blessed.
    (example: Blessed Revival Grass getting sealed by a Curse Girl)
  • The last item you throw at a Mixer when synthesizing determines the state of the resulting item.
    (example: Throw a cursed shield, then a normal shield, result will be normal)
  • Scoopies and Bored Kappas can't throw dirt or items into sealed pots.
    (Also, sealed Black Hole Pots will still create Pitfall Traps when shattered)
