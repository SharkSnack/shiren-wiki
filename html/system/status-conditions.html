Shiren 5 / Status Conditions

Status Conditions

Status conditions are states that affect a character's behavior in various ways.
All statuses besides "Regular" can be categorized as positive or negative.

Absorbiphant monsters and the Sparrow NPC can remove all status conditions
except for Super (and its boosts), Dueling, Dodger, Cautious, Reflective, and Zen.

For information about auras, check the Gameplay Basics page.

Shiren

  • Negative statuses can be removed by using items like Peach, Fixer Scroll, or Immunity Scroll.
    (Immune status will protect you from negative statuses)
  • You can check your current status from the menu. (Other → Current State)
  • Most status conditions wear off after a number of turns, or when you change floors.
    (However, Super status and corresponding bonuses will remain active)
  • Being revived by Revival Grass or Undo Grass will remove all status conditions
    except for Dodger, Cautious, Reflective, and Zen.

Allies

  • Negative statuses can be removed by using items like Peach or Immunity Scroll.
    (Immune status will protect the ally from negative statuses)

Monsters

  • Negative statuses can be removed by a Peach or Cure Monster Trap.
    (Immune status will protect the monster from negative statuses)
  • Nixer Scroll will have no effect if used on a monster that has a negative status.
