Starting Post-Game

The main story basically serves as a tutorial in the Shiren series, and post-game is where the "actual game" begins.

Preparations

  • Obtain Encyclopedia
    • Visit Ponta's Lot Shop in the basement of Hotel Nekomaneki to obtain the Encyclopedia.
      Having access to the Item Book makes naming unidentified items much easier.
  • Read Scrolls
    • You'll need to have read key scrolls like Extinction Scroll in order to make the most of Blank Scrolls in post-game dungeons.
      Extinction and Extraction scrolls can be found in Merchant's Hideout. (Or you can use Rescue Passwords to obtain them)
  • Learn Abilities
    • If you plan on challenging dungeons with night gameplay, you'll want to learn abilities like Stairlight and Pot Party at minimum.
      Note that the Master NPC can only appear between 1-3F of Tower of Fortune for main story dungeons.
      (See Abilities for information on how to learn each ability)
  • Collect Catstones
    • The Catstone selling NPC named Poo will begin to travel to dungeons you've entered before after clearing the main story.
      Poo will only visit Destiny Trail, Tower of the Past/Present/Future, and Tower of Fortune after a fresh story clear,
      so it's recommended that you don't enter post-game dungeons until you've stocked up on Catstones. (Dungeon Center is fine)
      (See Tips and Tricks for details)

Unlocking Dungeons

Most dungeons are available from the start at the Dungeon Center located in Nekomaneki Village.

  • Underground Manor (General District): Clear all 10 districts of Underground Manor.
  • Inori Cave: Inside the Beginner House in Inori Village after clearing the main story.
  • Lost Well: Visit the Sparrow's Inn after clearing the main story.
  • Gen's Turf: Give Catnip Juice to Gen (obtained in Inori Village), and return to his house after clearing the main story.
  • Pitfall of Life: Reach the top of Tower of Miracles with Kojirouta in your party after clearing the main story.
  • Heavenly Lake: Talk to Charon outside the Dungeon Center in Nekomaneki Village after clearing the main story.
  • Old Road: Level Okon and Gen to Lv10 and Koharu to Lv2, and then visit Hotel Nekomaneki â†’ Gen's House (check letter).
  • Bizarre Tower: Reach the top of Tower of Fortune with Tao in your party, meet Tao inside the Sparrow's Inn,
    reach the top of Tower of Miracles, and then talk to Tao at the Tower of Fortune Entrance.
  • Onigiri Hollow: Complete the Decchi sidequest. (See Onigiri Hollow for details)
  • Primordial Chasm: Visit the Hermit's Hermitage Blacksmith after clearing the main story.
  • Destiny's Descent: Wish to play a tougher dungeon in Inori Cave. (Use Tanuki Passwords for a set of Catstones)
  • Garden of Destiny: Available at the Dungeon Center after clearing the main story.

Recommended Dungeons

  • The most popular dungeons are Primordial Chasm (day only), Destiny's Descent (day/night),
    Master's Footprint (no healing items), and Perilous Rocks (lots of monster houses).
    • You'll need to clear the dungeon once to unlock the 99F version for all of these except Destiny's Descent.
    • Primordial Chasm in particular is a favorite among players / streamers.
  • Monster Hotspot and Underground Manor (General District) are good for practicing deeper runs.
  • Dungeons with special rules like Bizarre Tower (choose your own floors), Pitfall of Life (Shiren can't level up),
    and Onigiri Hollow (rooms collapse when you exit them) can be a fun way to shake things up.

Expert Badges

  • Some dungeons track their own achievements in the form of Expert Badges.
    They generally challenge you to clear a dungeon with restrictions, such as not using certain item categories.
