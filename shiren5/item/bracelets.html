Can. Arm Bracelet

Makes projectiles pierce through walls and creatures.
Powerful when used with arrows, Gitan, or talismans.
Items with piercing properties will hit monsters immune to projectiles.
However, be sure to unequip it while not in use to prevent accidents,
especially on floors where Swordsman, Gazer, and Field Knave monsters appear.

Versus Mode:
Equip this bracelet and throw an item toward a wall to send it at the other player. (can miss)
Anything that can be thrown (arrows, grass, Gitan, etc.) can hit the player,
so it's easily one of the most powerful items to turn the tide in this mode.
Unlike magic bullets from staves, thrown items can be sent even when
you're on different floors, making a thrown Swap Staff very effective.

Inacc. Bracelet

It seems like a negative item at first, but can be used to trap monsters using revealed traps.
Decay Trap can be used to create Rotten Onigiri, and Sleep Trap lets you defeat enemies
without taking damage, etc.

Strength Bracelet

Increases strength by 3 while equipped, making it easy to identify.
Nice to keep on hand for digging up Shiny Objects, similar to Shiren 4.
It shines in Pitfall of Life, since strength is more important when you can't level up.
Resonates with Counter Shield. (doubles counter damage)

Bunch Bracelet

Raises attack power if you're carrying items of the same type as your equipped weapon.
Excludes items in pots, and it's the only 2000G bracelet that can't be identified easily.
The attack power increase isn't very high, so it's usually not worth the inventory space.
However, each additional copy of the weapon type raises attack power further.

Staunch Bracelet

Nullifies effects that decrease max HP, max strength, max fullness, and level.
Includes Polygon Spinna, StunScorp, BlightScorp, MC Wizard, MC Sorceror,
Stomach Shrinker, Unlucky Seed, SuperUnlucky Seed.
These effects aren't too dire, so it doesn't see much use outside of Pitfall of Life.
Note: It doesn't prevent strength loss from Poison Grass, Poison Arrows, or Rotten Onigiri.

Mojo Bracelet

Direct attacks or projectiles can deal a critical hit if you're carrying cursed or sealed items.
Items in pots are excluded, and the chance increases with more items.
It limits inventory space, but it's among the strongest bracelets in the game.
Effects can be stacked using 2 bracelet resonance to raise critical hit rate further.
(1 bracelet = 18 items for 100%, 2 bracelets = 9 items for 100%)
Resonates with Violent Blade. (direct attacks occasionally inflict slow)

Formula:
Critical hit rate = 5xy + 10%, x = number of cursed or sealed items, y = number of bracelets

Trap Bracelet

New traps will be generated as turns pass while this bracelet is equipped. (max: 64)
The bracelet effect stacks, and is often used to collect points in Destiny Trail.
Perception Grass can be used with this bracelet to locate useful traps
in dungeons that don't allow carry-in items.

Dozer Bracelet

Inflicts Shiren with Asleep status every 10 steps while it's equipped.
Useful when you want to boost Okon's attack power. (Absorbiphant transformation)
Can be used with the "Increases HP healed while sleeping" New Item effect to
quickly regenerate HP, but the risk usually outweighs the benefit.

Heal Bracelet

Significantly speeds up hunger rate and HP regeneration rate while it's equipped.
Hunger rate is multiplied by 4.8, making it tricky to use even with the CR Diet rune,
but the ability to quickly recover from high damage encounters is worth the cost.
Remains in effect while Satiated, but no effect while Starving.

Incredible HP recovery item in Old Road, where fullness depletes in real time.
