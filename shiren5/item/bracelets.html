Receive 10 damage per turn when a monster is visible within scouting range.

Receive 10 damage every time you pick up an item.

Time Stop Bracelet × Stops the time of day from changing.
Floating Bracelet × Lets you walk on water and void tiles, and traps will no longer trigger.
Warps you to a different location if unequipped over a water or void tile.
Identify Bracelet 30000 10500 × Automatically identifies floor items you pick up or insert into pots.
Scout Bracelet × Reveals the location of monsters, NPCs, and items on the floor.
Trapper Bracelet × Lets you pick up traps as items, and you can trap monsters.
Night Ward ○ Increases regular attack damage against night monsters.
