Can be thrown at a creature for the same effect if capacity is above 0.
Zalokleft Pot Releases a Zalokleft that retreives an item up to 10 tiles ahead.
The Zalokleft can't pick up pots, and can't cross wall or air tiles.
Shatter the pot to retrieve the items the Zalokleft picked up.
If shattered while capacity is above 0, confused Zaloklefts will appear.
Monster Pot Open to make a number of monsters equal to capacity appear around you.
The monsters will appear while confused if shattered instead.
(Monsters are selected from the current floor's monster table)
Hilarious Pot 2~4 Inflicts Laughing status on a target in front of you.
Laughing status prevents the creature from performing any actions,
and the laughter travels to other nearby creatures.
Can be thrown at a creature for the same effect if capacity is above 0.
Klein Pot 3~5 Applies Klein status for 20 turns, swapping your attack and defense.
Can be thrown at a creature for the same effect if capacity is above 0.
Shrine Maid. Pot 3 Fully restores HP, strength, fullness, and cures all status ailments.
Also grants Swift status for 15 turns, and Invincible status for 20 turns.
Synthesis Pot 5 6000 300 2100 105 Insert same category items to combine them into a single item.
The first item acts as the base, and items afterward are consumed.
Weapons and shields transfer abilities (runes) and upgrade value,
and staves have their use counts added together.
Items with a price tag in stores can't be synthesized.
Modder's Pot 3~5 Same as Synthesis Pot, but the pot can't shatter.
It won't break even if sealed, and the pot will vanish if thrown at a creature.
Upgrade Pot 2~3 10000 500 3500 175 Inserted equipment, staves, and Gitan upgrade when you change floors.
Equipment upgrade value and staff use count increases by 1,
and the amount of Gitan inside the pot increases.
High chance to automatically shatter after changing floors.
Automatically shatters if an inserted item reaches its upgrade limit.
Degrade Pot Inserted equipment, staves, and Gitan degrade when you change floors.
Equipment upgrade value and staff use count decreases by 1,
and the amount of Gitan inside the pot decreases.
Lucky Pot 3~5 Chance for items to be blessed when you change floors while carrying this.
Unlucky Pot Chance for items to be cursed when you change floors while carrying this.
Grilling Pot Inserted onigiri turn into Grilled Onigiri.
Aksys Pot 1 45000 2250 8320 416 Open to listen to a message from Aksys Games. (Famitsu in JP verison)

Preservation Pot

Pot that lets you insert and take out items freely.
Onigiri inside the pot won't be grilled or spoiled, and peaches won't ripen.
Once identified, you can change the name of the pot at will, which is helpful
when storing items in Nekomaneki Village or inside storage buildings.
Insert Gitan to use as projectiles, protect staves from Gyadon's pecking, etc.

Ordinary Pot

No special effects.
Contents can only be taken out by breaking the pot or using an Extraction Scroll.
Recommended to insert important items that you don't need to use right away,
such as Mixer synthesis ingredients or high value items to sell in shops.
Another option is to intentionally fill the pot with Dirt from a Scoopie.

Hide Pot

Grants Hiding status for 20 turns, making enemies unable to detect you.
Inflicts Hiding status when thrown, trapping the creature until they're attacked.
Instant game over if Shiren enters the pot while standing on a water or air tile.
Can't be filled with Dirt from Scoopie special attacks.

Shiren can still enter the pot if its capacity is reduced to 0 by a Gyandora,
but a Hide Pot [0] won't inflict Hiding status when thrown at a target.
Talking to a hiding ally cancels their Hiding status.

4-2-8 Pot

Causes a big explosion to occur when it shatters. (1 tile radius)
Sealing the pot will prevent it from exploding, allowing you to safely retrieve contents.
Shiren's current HP will be reduced to 1, and all enemies caught in the blast vanish.
If thrown with piercing properties, each target it hits will explode.
All other 4-2-8 Pots will be identified when one explodes.
One of the few items that can one-shot Gyazas.

The name is a reference to 428: Shibuya Scramble.

Sale Pot

Transforms inserted items into Gitan equal to their selling price.
The maximum amount per item is 9999G, and it also works on merchandise.
Gitan inside the Sale Pot won't be included when you sell the pot in a dungeon,
but will be included if sold at a shop located in a village.

Lets you recover from a Bankruptcy Scroll, but it's dangerous to carry it on floors
where Gazers appear since they can make you insert equipped items.

Buy/+1 1000 / 50 Rune - Item
Book		 Items inserted into this pot will be
converted into their Gitan value.

Presto Pot

Transforms inserted items into other items. (no effect on merchandise)
Recommended to insert a single arrow or rock at a time.
Presto Pots have a unique item table, but you generally can't obtain anything
too special except for Kabura Katana or Fuuma Shield in select dungeons.

Be careful with your selection, since it's easy to insert equipped items by mistake.
Similar to Shiren 4, inserted items can't transform into pots in this game.

Inflicts Shiren with Potted status when you try to insert an item,
unequipping your weapon and preventing you from using items or abilities.
If you have Immune status, your hand won't get stuck. (weapon is still unequipped)
Potted status is canceled when you change floors or step on a Strip Trap.
Chance to break free when you attack or get hit by enemies.

It's mostly a negative item, but can be used to unequip a cursed weapon.

Unbreakable Pot

Pot that can't break, even when thrown against a wall.
It won't break even if sealed, and the pot and its contents is lost if thrown at an enemy.
The only way to retrieve its contents is to use an Extraction Scroll.
Can be used to store Revival Grass or Undo Grass if you don't plan on blessing them,
but otherwise the only practical use for it is to insert items to be sold later.
