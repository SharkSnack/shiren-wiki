Pot Summary

Name Size Buy +1 Sell +1 Info
Preservation Pot 3~5 600 30 210 10.5 Insert and take out items freely.
Can be labeled after being identified.
Ordinary Pot No effect.
Identify Pot Identifies inserted items.
Hide Pot Select insert to hide inside the pot for 20 turns.
Can be thrown to trap an enemy inside the pot.
The pot only shatters if it's hit by an effect or attack.
You'll fail the adventure if you hide while on a water or air tile.
4-2-8 Pot Explodes when it shatters, and any items inside will be lost.
Other 4-2-8 Pots will be identified after one explodes.
Use an Extraction Scroll or seal the pot to retrieve contents safely.
However, note that blessed 4-2-8 pots can't be sealed later.
Sale Pot 3~5 1000 50 350 17.5 Transforms inserted items into Gitan equal to their selling price.
Dirt inserted by Scoopies will turn into grass items like normal.
Presto Pot Transforms inserted items into different items.
Items with a price tag in stores will not be transformed.
Sticky Pot Your hand gets stuck inside the pot when you try to insert an item.
If that happens, shatter the pot by attacking or getting hit by enemies.
Strip Trap, Absorbiphants, and changing floors will also free your hand.
Unbreakable Pot Pot that can't shatter, even when sealed.
The only way to take items out is to use an Extraction Scroll.
The pot and all items inside vanish when thrown at a creature.
Can be used as a container for Revival Grass or Undo Grass.
Floramorph Pot Transforms inserted items into different types of flowers.
Flowers are registered to the Item Book, but can't be used as items.
Black Hole Pot 2~4 Inserted items vanish.
If shattered, a number of Pit Traps equal to the size of the pot appear.
Pit Traps appear even if it's sealed, so seal it to counter Gazer hypnosis.
Fever Pot 3 Duplicates inserted items when you insert identical items to capacity.
Upgrade value, blessed, cursed, and sealed states are ignored.
Duplicated items won't be blessed, cursed, or sealed.
Equipment will have default runes and an upgrade value of 0.
Staves have a random use count, and projectiles have a random quantity.
Minimum pot size to take effect is [2].
The pot will explode if different items are inserted.
Exorcism Pot 2~4 1600 80 560 28 Removes curses and seals from inserted items.
Blessing Pot Blesses inserted items.
Curse Pot Curses or seals inserted items.
Water Pot 3~5 2000 100 700 35 Fill it with water, and splash water 1 tile in the direction you're facing.
Splashed water rusts equipment, interacts with gimmicks, ripens peaches,
destroys traps, weakens some monsters, etc. (See details for full list)
Heavenly Pot 3 Fully restores HP, and cures most status ailments.
Fill it at the Inori Village hot spring.
Zen Pot 2~4 2500 125 875 43.75 Insert an item to apply Zen status to all creatures on the current floor.
Zen status nullifies all damage from explosions and fire.
The inserted item for any of the 4 pots in this price range will vanish.
Dodger Pot Insert an item to apply Dodger status to all creatures on the current floor.
Dodger status causes all arrows and thrown items to miss.
Note that Mixers will still swallow thrown items.
Perceptive Pot Insert an item to apply Cautious status to all creatures on the current floor.
Cautious status prevents dropping items, and protects you from theft.
(Zalokleft, Froggo, Gyadon, Swordsman, Field Knave, Trip Stone)
Enemies can still drop items when defeated.
Reflection Pot Insert an item to apply Reflective status to all creatures on the current floor.
Reflective status causes all magic to be reflected.
Heal Pot 3~5 3500 175 1225 61.25 Restores HP and strength to max, and cures some status ailments.
Can be thrown at a creature for the same effect if capacity is above 0.
Zalokleft Pot Releases a Zalokleft that retreives an item up to 10 tiles ahead.
The Zalokleft can't pick up pots, and can't cross wall or air tiles.
Shatter the pot to retrieve the items the Zalokleft picked up.
If shattered while capacity is above 0, confused Zaloklefts will appear.
Monster Pot Open to make a number of monsters equal to capacity appear around you.
The monsters will appear while confused if shattered instead.
(Monsters are selected from the current floor's monster table)
Hilarious Pot 2~4 Inflicts Laughing status on a target in front of you.
Laughing status prevents the creature from performing any actions,
and the laughter travels to other nearby creatures.
Can be thrown at a creature for the same effect if capacity is above 0.
Klein Pot 3~5 Applies Klein status for 20 turns, swapping your attack and defense.
Can be thrown at a creature for the same effect if capacity is above 0.
Shrine Maid. Pot 3 Fully restores HP, strength, fullness, and cures all status ailments.
Also grants Swift status for 15 turns, and Invincible status for 20 turns.
Synthesis Pot 5 6000 300 2100 105 Insert same category items to combine them into a single item.
The first item acts as the base, and items afterward are consumed.
Weapons and shields transfer abilities (runes) and upgrade value,
and staves have their use counts added together.
Items with a price tag in stores can't be synthesized.
Modder's Pot 3~5 Same as Synthesis Pot, but the pot can't shatter.
It won't break even if sealed, and the pot will vanish if thrown at a creature.
Upgrade Pot 2~3 10000 500 3500 175 Inserted equipment, staves, and Gitan upgrade when you change floors.
Equipment upgrade value and staff use count increases by 1,
and the amount of Gitan inside the pot increases.
High chance to automatically shatter after changing floors.
Automatically shatters if an inserted item reaches its upgrade limit.
Degrade Pot Inserted equipment, staves, and Gitan degrade when you change floors.
Equipment upgrade value and staff use count decreases by 1,
and the amount of Gitan inside the pot decreases.
Lucky Pot 3~5 Chance for items to be blessed when you change floors while carrying this.
Unlucky Pot Chance for items to be cursed when you change floors while carrying this.
Grilling Pot Inserted onigiri turn into Grilled Onigiri.
Aksys Pot 1 45000 2250 8320 416 Open to listen to a message from Aksys Games. (Famitsu in JP verison)
Exclusive to Shiren 5 Plus, and doesn't appear in the Item Book.
