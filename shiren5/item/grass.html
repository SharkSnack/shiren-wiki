Weeds

No effect when eaten, other than restoring 5 fullness like all other grass items.
Increases action speed of Field Knave family monsters by 1 stage if thrown at them.
Synthesize with a weapon to create the Anti-Plant rune.

Obtained from Field Knaves (special attack or drop), used Revival Grass or Undo Grass,
allies getting hit by Swordsman special attacks, or from a Secret Pot after 8 days pass.

Herb

Restores 25 HP, or increases max HP by 1 if already at max.
It's best to wait until your HP is full to increase max HP early ~ mid game,
but it's fine to use it right away late game, since you don't have time to step in place.
Synthesize with a weapon or shield to create the Lively rune.

An Herb is placed where a New Item would've been generated when
you don't have any New Items registered to your encyclopedia.

Poison Grass

Deals 5 damage, decreases strength by 1, and slows action speed by 1 stage for 10 turns.
Inflicts Weakened status and slows action speed by 1 stage if thrown at an enemy.
However, Mutaikons and Scorpions will gain Buffed status instead.
Effects can be nullified by a Cleansing Bracelet.

Mutaikon family monsters throw their respective grass + Poison Grass.
Mutaikons can only drop Poison Grass when defeated.

Restores 200 HP and cures Confused, Blind, and Slow statuses.
Increases max HP by 3 if already at max.
Reliable recovery item even in end game floors, and it's worth using

Synthesize with a weapon or shield to create the Vitalizing rune.

Upgrade Seed

Grants Tinkerer status for 100 turns. (Skill points from monsters is multiplied by 4)
Tinkerer status remains active until you change floors if it was blessed.
It's best to save it for floors where high skill point monsters like Dagger Bee appear,
and try to bless it before using it, especially in Pitfall of Life.
Deals 2 damage when thrown.

Synthesize a sword with this + Oil Scroll + Explosion Bracelet to create the Blue Flame rune.

Perception Grass

Grants Farseeing status for the duration of the current floor,
which lets you see hidden traps and read scrolls without light at night.
This effect is cancelled if you get inflicted by Blind status.
Applies Farseeing status to the target when thrown.

Synthesize with a weapon to create the Anti-Cyclops rune.
Synthesize 2 Perception Grass with a shield to create the Agile rune.

Swift Grass

Grants Swift status for 14 turns. (Increases action speed by 1 stage)
Using Swift Grass won't end your turn, and it can't be absorbed by Absorbiphants.
Increases the target's action speed by 1 stage when thrown.
Fantastic for boss fights and escaping tricky situations.

Synthesize a sword with 2 from the following items to create the Quick Hitting rune:
Swift Grass, Swift Staff, or Swift Talisman.
