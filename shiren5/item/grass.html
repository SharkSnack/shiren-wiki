Fantastic if you're surrounded or when you're facing high power monsters.

Unlike Glorious Staff and Glorious Talisman, it's not based on experience points.
Madam Ateska can force it to appear on the ground in select dungeons.
Shiren won't level up if used in Pitfall of Life.

Synthesize with a weapon or shield to create the Enhancing rune.

Amnesia Grass

Makes you forget all of the items you've identified during the current adventure.
This effect is undoubtedly bad, but it has a low chance of being generated
and can only be found in the shop table of 2 dungeons in the base game.
If you're truly worried, you could take notes as you play, but it's a bit overkill.
Inflicts Apathetic status for 6 turns when thrown at an enemy.

Imabikiso

All inventory items become cursed or sealed when used.
Inflicts Sealed status if thrown at an enemy.
If thrown at a Curse Girl family monster, they will level up instead.
Useful if you have a Mojo Bracelet, but otherwise it's an extremely dangerous item.

Angel Seed

Increases level by 3. (6 if blessed)
Useful for leveling up ally characters.
It's best to duplicate them using Fever Pots, and then create New Items out of them.
You could also synthesize it to increase your weapon or shield's base stat by 8.
Madam Ateska can force it to appear on the ground in select dungeons.

Synthesize with a weapon or shield to create the Augmenting rune.

SuperUnlucky Seed

Reduces level and current HP to 1, and sets fullness to 0.
However, eating grass replenishes 5 fullness, so you won't collapse right away.
Be mindful that items dropped by night monsters are selected from the shop table.
It's a disasterous item, but the effect can be nullified by a Staunch Bracelet.

Synthesize with a weapon or shield to create the Perilous rune.
