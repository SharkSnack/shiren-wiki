Gut Grass - - Revives you if it's in your inventory when you collapse,
but immediately makes you collapse again.
Undo Grass 1500 250 - - Grants an option to either revive (Revival Grass effect)
or escape from the dungeon with your items if you collapse.
Only grants an option to escape from the dungeon if eaten.
Undo Grass is prioritzed if you're also carrying Revival Grass.
Repeat Grass - - Prompts you in a similar manner to Undo Grass,
but won't actually let you revive or escape from the dungeon.
Invincible Grass 2000 700 - - Applies Invincible status for 20 turns.

Levels up Curse Girls if thrown at them.
