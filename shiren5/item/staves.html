Pinning Staff

Shiren leaps forward to the wall or creature the magic bullet hit.
Great for run-away play, stealing, and taking shortcuts in general.
When leaping to walls, you can throw an item first to check for traps on the destination tile.
Gyazas convert magic bullets to 2 damage, so they can't be used as a destination target.

Statue Cave will introduce you to some of this staff's common use cases.

Slow Staff

Decreases the target's action speed by 1 stage. (See Slow status)
The effect lasts for the duration of the current floor when swung at an enemy.
Loop attack → step away → attack to defeat enemies without taking damage,
provided they only have attacks that hit adjacently or in a straight line.
Also useful for Mixer synthesis.

Action speed stages (enemy):
Swift (2 attacks) → Swift (1 attack) → Normal → Slow
※ Yellow auras increase action speed by 2 stages.

Swift Staff

Increases the target's action speed by 1 stage. (See Swift status)

Clone Staff

Inflicts Cloned status, making enemies attack the target instead of Shiren or allies.
Similar to Shiren 4, enemies will only go after Fake Shiren if they're within view.
Only 1 Fake Shiren can be present at a time, and the previous character
will be left Confused if a new Fake Shiren is created.
Monsters that defeat Fake Shiren will level up.

If both Chow and Fake Shiren are present in a room, enemies will prioritze Chow.
Unlike Sealed status, monster special abilities can't be shut down by Cloned status.
(example: Mixers will still swallow items while Cloned)

Fort. Staff

Creates a wall 1 tile ahead, or deals 2 damage if thrown or an enemy is in front of you.
Perform a direct attack to destroy the wall and change it to a regular floor tile.
(example: create a wall on a water tile near an item island, destroy the wall, cross over)
Also useful for creating safe spaces when cycling day and night, stealing, etc.
If you enter a created wall using a Wall Clip Bracelet, the wall will collapse.

Synthesize this + Immunity Scroll + Explosion Bracelet with a shield for the Anti-Blast※ rune.

Sharing Staff

Inflicts Shiren with Empathetic status, taking damage equal to damage dealt to the target.
However, you won't receive damage if the target is defeated in a single hit.
Since it's an ailment, Super status can wear off when it's used.
It's a negative item, but worth keeping for its powerful rune.
