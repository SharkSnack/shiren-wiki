Conf. Talisman

Inflicts Confused status on a target and its surroundings when thrown. (1 tile radius)
Easiest to use among the common talismans. (shadow bind, confusion, seal, and sleep)
There's some risk of monsters leveling up or hitting you with direct attacks, but the
confusion lasts 20 turns and is generally reliable for overcoming confrontations.

Seal Talisman

Inflicts Sealed status on a target and its surroundings when thrown. (1 tile radius)
It's better to use staves or scrolls if a monster with a dangerous special attack
is adjacent to you, since thrown items only have 84% accuracy in this game.

Fear Talisman

Inflicts Afraid status on a target and its surroundings when thrown. (1 tile radius)
Lasts 15 turns which is less than Conf. Talisman, but has the advantage of predictability.
It's a bit rarer than the 4 common talismans. (shadow bind, confusion, seal, sleep)
Can be used to level up monsters.

Inacc. Talisman

Inflicts Inaccurate status on a target and its surroundings when thrown. (1 tile radius)
Lasts 10 turns, and doesn't prevent monsters from using their special attacks.
Nice when you want Floaty family monsters to throw you to another floor,
or when you want a Nigiri Morph to change an item into a Large Onigiri, etc.

Buy 600 Duration 10 Turns Item
Book		 Throw this at a target to afflict all
creatures in the area with
Inaccurate status.
(Creatures afflicted with Inaccurate
status will always miss their attacks.
Special attacks will still hit.)

Sleep Talisman

Inflicts Asleep status on a target and its surroundings when thrown. (1 tile radius)
Completely shuts down all targets, making it the best talisman on paper.
However, in practice you'll find that groups of enemies or naturally Swift enemies
will often wake up before you have a chance to defeat them, since it only lasts 6 turns.
