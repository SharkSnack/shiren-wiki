Rotten Onigiri

Restores 30 fulless, but also deals 5 damage and one of the following negative effects:
Inflict Confused, Asleep, Blind, or decrease strength by 3. (Can be blocked with bracelets)
All onigiri in your inventory will turn into Rotten Onigiri when you step on a Decay Trap,
and Rotten Onigiri can be turned into Grilled Onigiri by getting hit by fire or explosion damage.
Dropped by Foul Nigiri Baby family monsters.

Throw it at a Nigiri Baby family monster to instantly defeat them.
Throw it at a Kid Squid family monster to turn them into a Squid Sushi Scroll.
※ Doesn't work if the onigiri item is sealed.

Grilled Onigiri

Restores 60 fullness and 40 HP. (Increases max fullness by 3 if already full)
All onigiri in your inventory will be grilled if you get hit by fire or explosion damage,
excluding onigiri inside pots. Any seals or blessings will be removed when that happens.
※ Grilled Onigiri are already grilled, so they can't be grilled to remove seals.

It's best to eat it when fullness has depleted by at least 60, and after receiving damage.
Unlike previous games, Grilled Onigiri can turn into Rotten Onigiri.

Throw it at a Nigiri Baby family monster to instantly defeat them.
Throw it at a Kid Squid family monster to turn them into a Squid Sushi Scroll.
※ Doesn't work if the onigiri item is sealed.

Special Onigiri

Restores 30 fullness, and receive one of the following positive effects:
Unwavering, Satiated, Ninja, Insomnia, Knowledgeable, heal HP, or increase strength by 1.
Special Onigiri are shop-exclusive, and will increase max fullness by 2 if already full.
It's best to wait until you have multiple Special Onigiri and are at full HP when aiming
for Knowledgeable status, since you won't get repeat effects.

Throw it at a Nigiri Baby family monster to instantly defeat them.
Throw it at a Kid Squid family monster to turn them into a Squid Sushi Scroll.
※ Doesn't work if the onigiri item is sealed.

Juicy Peach

Restores 100 fullness, and replenishes 8 used abilities.
Also grants Enduring status, which lets you survive a fatal hit with 1 HP remaining.
A blessed Juicy Peach lets you survive 2 fatal hits instead of a single hit.
Be careful near Absorbiphant family monsters, since they can remove Enduring status.

Rotten Peach

Restores 15 fullness, and replenishes 1 used ability.
Also inflicts Weakened and Wizened status, lowering attack and defense.
If you must eat it due to a lack of food, do so near the stairs right before changing floors.
Otherwise, you can throw it at a monster to weaken them similar to a Poison Arrow.
