Shiren 5 / Necklace Abilities

Necklace Abilities

Necklace abilities, also called techniques, are attacks that can only be used at night.
They are the primary means to deal damage to night monsters,
and are first used in Tower of Fortune during the main story.

Up to 8 abilities can be assigned to the necklace, including multiples of the same ability.
Wandering NPCs can teach you new abilities, and you can swap abilities at Hotel Nekomaneki.
Some abilities can only be learned by repeatedly using specific abilities.

With the exception of Staccato Wave, abilities can only be used one time each floor.
It's possible to replenish moves by eating peaches, reading a Replenish Scroll,
being revived by Revival Grass / Undo Grass, or the Ability Regen rune taking effect.

Attack power of abilities increases as Shiren levels up or gains the Super status.
Most abilities deal 2 damage to shopkeepers, but status effects still work on them.

Electrocute A (60) Level 16 Electric damage to a target in front, which travels and damages all adjacent targets.

Powerful when used alongside Crawling Confusion.
Concussive Cannon B (50) Level 20 Shoots a piercing 3 tile wide blast in a straight line, damaging all enemies in its path.
It can defeat multiple monsters, but the lack of power becomes noticeable mid game.
Bi-Directional Blast B (50) Master Shoots a bullet ahead and behind you, each bullet damaging the first enemy it hits.
The ability will instantly defeat both enemies if you manage to land both bullets.
However, it's difficult to do so since night monsters move in unpredictable ways.
Death-go-Round B (40) Level 32 Damages enemies in a 1 tile radius. Damage dealt increases with more targets.

Can be used to obtain Rotten Onigiri (Water Pot) and Grilled Onigiri (Dragon Grass)
Dicey Distractor B (30) Tradesman Damages enemies in a 1 tile radius, and then inflicts Afraid status.
Night monsters can still hit you if they aim for a monster instead of Shiren.
Room Cyclone B (30) Tradesman Damages all enemies in the room.
The ability was nerfed from Shiren 4 (40 power), so it's not as useful in Shiren 5.
Injury to Insult B (30) Derived from
HP Divider		 Damages all enemies in the room. Deals x1.5 damage to enemies with an ailment.
Basically a better Room Cyclone.
Darkroom C (20) Derived from
Room Cyclone		 Damages all enemies in the room, and then inflicts Blind status.
Practically no reason to use this over Pot Party.
Pot Party C (20) Master Damages all enemies in the room, and then inflicts Hiding status.
Extremely powerful ability, since monsters can't break out of the pot on their own.
However, be careful of Absorbiphants and Flamebirds.
Room Sealant C (20) Apprentice Damages all enemies in the room, and then inflicts Sealed status.
Staccato Wave C (20) Sage in Hermit's
Hermitage		 Damages an enemy in front. Can be used an unlimited number of times.
It only deals ~20 damage, but can be nice for early game or if you gain Super status.
Self-Help - Level 24 Grants Fast Regen status. (+1 HP recovered per turn, remains active during the day)
Mostly used to support daytime fights, since you want to avoid damage at night.
Chakra Cleanse - Level 29 Auto-activates when you become afflicted with an ailment during the night.
Any ailment you become afflicted with will be instantly cured,
so it's excellent to have in case you step on a trap at night.
Beacon of Light - Level 55 Grants Night Vision status, allowing you to read scrolls without light.
Three Screams - Level 99 Instantly defeats enemies in front of you in 3 forward directions,
but you won't gain experience points. Can hit enemies around corners.
Unlike Shiren 4, this ability can't defeat shopkeepers in Shiren 5.
Transmogrification - Inori Cave (wish) Instantly defeats an enemy in front of you by transforming them into an item.
You won't gain experience points, and it doesn't work on shopkeepers.
The item is selected from the dungeon's floor item table, with some exceptions:
・Monsters with fixed item drops will always drop that item.
・Mixers, Zaloklefts, and Froggos will drop what they swallowed or stole.
Items generated by this move will never be blessed, cursed, or sealed.
(This includes normally always cursed items like Nonary Bracelet and Itemphobic)
Last Stand - Apprentice 80% chance to instantly defeat all monsters on the floor, but you collapse if it fails.
You won't gain any experience points, and it always fails if there are no monsters.
Monsters can still drop items when they're defeated by this ability.
The effect looks like an explosion, but items and walls won't be destroyed.
Decent for dungeons that allow carry-in items. (Bring lots of Revival Grass)
Pyrrhic Piercer - Apprentice Shoots a piercing bullet in a straight line that reduces HP to 1.
Shiren's HP will also be halved, and the bullet can't pass through walls.
HP Divider - Apprentice Halves the HP of all enemies in a room, and defeats them if they were at 1 HP.
Room Healing Flash - Tradesman Fully restores HP of you and any allies in the room.
Excellent to have when rushing stairs / speed running.
Crawling Confusion - Tradesman Gathers monsters on the current floor to you, and then inflicts Confused status.
Monsters that didn't warp due to lack of space will also be left confused.
Handy, but also risky since confused enemies can still hit you.
Reflecto - Master Reflects all direct attacks for 1 turn. Damage is equal to what you would've received.
Stairlight - Master Reveals the location of the stairs on the map.
Always include this ability, unless you're intentionally omitting it for a restricted play.
Jack in the Box - Clear Night
Training Facility		 Randomly performs an ability from all of the above abilities.
Support abilities and Last Stand can be chosen, so it's not recommended to use this.

Formations

Formations are special effects that occur based on character positions when an ability is used.
When multiple formation conditions are met at the same time, the higher priority formation is chosen.
Some formations only activate with abilities of a specific category.

Priority Condition Effect Category

2 5 Line Restore 200 HP Room
3 3 Line Restore 50 HP Room
4 2 Line Cure ailments Room
5 Cross Replenish 3 abilities Room, Floor
6 Sandwich Restore 20 HP Room
