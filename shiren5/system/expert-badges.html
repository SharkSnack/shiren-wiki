Safe and Unsafe Actions

○ : Safe, × : Unsafe, ? : Unverified

No equipping weapons, shields, or bracelets
○ Equip a torch. (No weapons)
× Equip an item thrown by a Bored Kappa monster.
No food
○ Eat grass.
○ Throw a food item.
○ Read a Fixer Scroll when you're starving to restore fullness.
○ Restore fullness by reviving using Revival Grass, Undo Grass, or Wanderer Rescue.
○ Restore fullness by getting hit by a peach thrown by a Bored Kappa monster.
○ Restore fullness by throwing a peach at a Bored Kappa monster and getting hit by the returned peach.
× Eat an onigiri, peach, or Squid Sushi Scroll.
No grass, scrolls, staves, talismans, or pots. (shared actions)
○ Pick up the item.
○ Place the item on the ground. (Selling the item is also safe)
○ Insert the item into a pot. (Excluding no pots)
○ Throw a pot containing the item. (Excluding no pots)
○ Selecting the item when reading a scroll. (Excluding no scrolls)
○ Get hit by an item thrown by a Bored Kappa or Grass Boy monster.
○ Performing Mixer synthesis using a Cloned status Mixer and a Bored Kappa monster.
○ N'dubba monster revealing itself when it was disguised as restricted category item.
? Identify a restricted category item using an Identify Bracelet or Special Onigiri effect.
× Throw the item.
× Throw a N'dubba disguised as a restricted category item.
× Being hypnotized by a Gazer monster and using the item.
× Equip the item. (No staves) (PS Vita version is safe)
No grass
○ Get hit by grass thrown by a Mutaikon monster.
○ Get hit by grass thrown or spread by a Grass Boy monster.
○ Another monster gets hit by grass thrown or spread by a Grass Boy monster.
× Be revived by the Boy's Sister NPC. (She throws Warp Grass at you)
No scrolls
○ Write on a Blank Scroll.
○ Show a Recommend. Letter to a Shopkeep.
? Show a Commend. Letter to a Shopkeep.
× Have a scroll stick to the ground. (Tripping, enemy drop, and Bored Kappa throw) (PS Vita version is safe)
× Show an Identify Scroll to a Shopkeep.
No pots
○ Get hit by a pot thrown by a Bored Kappa monster. (Only if the pot has no effect when shattered)
○ Hide inside a pot after getting hit by a Hide Pot thrown by a Bored Kappa monster.
○ 4-2-8 Pot explodes after being thrown by a Bored Kappa monster.
○ Use the Pot Party ability.
○ Monster falling into an abyss or getting submerged after using Pot Party.
× 4-2-8 Pot explodes as a result of tripping.
× Scoopie monster throwing dirt into a pot.
× Bored Kappa monster throwing an item into a pot.
× "Open" type pot (size 1+) shattering after being thrown by a Bored Kappa monster. (PS Vita version is safe)
× Black Hole Pot shattering after being thrown by a Bored Kappa monster. (PS Vita version is safe)
No synthesizing
○ Insert an item into a Synthesis Pot. (As long as nothing is synthesized)
× Synthesize staves.
× Mixer monster swallows an item. (Reported that it's safe if nothing is synthesized)
No collapsing
× Reviving using Revival Grass or Undo Grass.
× Reviving using Wanderer Rescue.
× Being revived by the Boy's Sister NPC.
Clear with 3 allies
○ Clear the dungeon while Koharu is transformed into equipment.
No direct attacks
○ Defeat enemies using abilities at night.
○ Destroy a wall using a pickaxe weapon.
○ Reflect damage using a Counter Shield.
○ Reflect damage using a shield with the Retribution rune.
○ An ally attacks an enemy.
○ Deal fire damage using Burning Blade or a Flame Shot type rune.
× Using a direct attack when hypnotized or inflicted with Berserk status.
No breaking walls
○ Pop Tank, Explochin, or Sweet Nut monster explosions destroying walls.
○ Get knocked into a wall by a Yanpii monster tackle.
○ Show a Recommend. Letter to a Shopkeep to open an elite store.
○ Give Digger Don a pickaxe weapon to have him dig walls.
× Dig walls using a pickaxe weapon or Boring Staff.
× Destroy a wall that was created by a Fort. Staff.
× Open an elite store by punching the entrance.
× Destroy a wall using an Explosion or Big Explosion trap.
× Destroy a wall by throwing a 4-2-8 Pot and creating an explosion.
× Destroy a wall using an explosion from an Explosion Bracelet.
