Shiren 5 / Resonance

Resonance

Resonance is a special effect that occurs when you equip specific combinations of equipment at the same time.

When a resonance effect activates, a message is displayed in the scrolling messages window,
and information about the effect and trigger condition gets added to the in-game item descriptions.
